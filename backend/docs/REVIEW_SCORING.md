# Review Scoring Engines

There are 10 distinct, modular scoring engines. Each engine has exactly one responsibility.

1. **IntentEngine**: Intent Match
2. **KeywordEngine**: Keyword Coverage and placement
3. **SEOEngine**: Title Length, Tags
4. **ReadabilityEngine**: Sentence length and flow
5. **GrammarEngine**: Grammar, punctuation
6. **NaturalnessEngine**: Human-like writing
7. **CTAEngine**: Presence and strength of Calls To Action
8. **AudienceEngine**: Tone matching
9. **ClickbaitEngine**: Overpromising, fake urgency
10. **PolicyEngine**: Platform compliance and restricted words (Fails immediately zero out the overall score).
